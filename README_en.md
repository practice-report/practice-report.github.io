   Properties of Project Metamorphosis

The project proceeds from a working hypothesis about the possibility of the
metamorphosis (transformation) of a researcher. Metamorphosis occurs when the
thinking of the researcher changes its aggregate state, transforming into a
different kind (mode). The initial (starting) mode of thinking, with which the
researcher is born into this world, is not something completed and final, but
represents an intermediate temporary form in the universal process of life.

Within the framework of Project Metamorphosis, this initial mode of thinking is
named "default". The surrounding world for such a sequential subject-object
mode of thinking appears as an alien environment, barely suitable for life,
which makes sense to seize, secure, and process for oneself.

In Project Metamorphosis, such behavior is described as path_1, having beneath
it a foundation in the form of fear. Where fear is the basic and fundamental
mechanism of survival. Fear is not an enemy to be fought, but a natural and
necessary mechanism having a limited term of application. The initial mode of
"default" is a prolonged use of this mechanism by the human, needed only in the
first stages, when life is still very weak and strives to establish itself in a
world difficult for survival.

The human has established dominance on the planet, but continues to use the
basic survival mechanism. This generates a conflict. The conflict consists in
the fact that the human continues to perceive the world as something hostile,
and themselves – as something that must be preserved and secured from the world.
Such behavior of the human in relation to the world is a consequence of the
work of the basic survival mechanism. All human activity, all that they produce
and are – is proceeds from fear, and is built upon it.

The prolonged use of the basic survival mechanism leads to the accumulation in 
the human of layers consisting of compressed fear and its derivatives. 
Such layers do not die with the physical body, but through a complex network 
of interactions are inherited by newly born people. Thus, every person at 
birth has a certain baggage formed before them: they are simultaneously both 
the bearer of the baggage, and its realization.

The layers form a filter which divides and passes through itself only separate
streams of the universal flow of being. Combinations of separate streams create
the individual flow of life of every person.

The filter-construct is stable, its main task is survival. The human serves the
construction, providing themselves as a resource, spending their life and
limited forces on protecting this filter-construct, more and more complicating
it, building up new layers.

The filter-construct is what is called the "I" (hereinafter "i-filter").

It is difficult and costly to transform the i-filter – both physically and
psychologically. The only possibility to do this is to stop "feeding" the
filter-construct with oneself. Project Metamorphosis is a tool actively using
such a possibility. Thus, the working hypothesis of the project is the
possibility of transforming the i-filter into a different aggregate state.

Verification of this hypothesis occurs individually. It can be proven or
refuted in only one way – through experience. It is impossible to
transfer this proof to another person through words – experience is not
transmitted by words. However, by words can be transmitted the understanding
about the very possibility of the metamorphosis of thinking, about the method
and difficulties on the path of such transformation.

The project-tool has in its structure ways of bypassing traps and dead ends
typical for self-investigation. However, this does not cancel the independence
and individual responsibility of the researcher. The project gives no answers,
gives no support, provides no "fast food", but is an effective tool transmitted
by one researcher to another equal researcher. Thus, the project has no
hierarchy of "knowing/not knowing".

A feature of the other type of thinking is absence as such: absence of benefit,
absence of protection, absence of the i-filter, absence of fear, absence of
definiteness, etc. This feature is the most significant obstacle. For the mode
of thinking "default", accumulation and achievement are characteristic. Loss
and absence for such a mode are equivalent to failure, danger, meaninglessness,
and death.

States of "non-duality", "bliss", "enlightenment" and other phenomena – are not
the final result. These are stages, behind which there is always unexplored
space. Stopping the investigation turns the entire traveled path into a dead
end. The investigation continues as long as the researcher is alive.

The project is written from the "other" side of the metamorphosis process, from
where all mechanisms, the entire "map" are "visible". Otherwise the project-
tool could not have been provided. The project is an immediate "imprint" of the
process lying at its basis. This does not make the project "truth", but leaves
it as a hypothesis, which requires no faith, but can be verified.

Project Metamorphosis is an autonomous artifact. It "consists" of understanding.
Understanding is not stored between words, but is located in the one who reads
the project or uses it. The project introduces nothing "of itself" into the
consciousness of the reader, but is a possibility (method + map) to see that
which is already there.

The project-tool has a full correspondence of form, structure, and content.
The project has no formal author (a legal waiver of authorship and rights is 
formalized through the CC0 license); the project is in the public domain, 
with no possibility of feedback, donations, etc. It contains no motivation or 
any other form of manipulation or violence. The residual significance of the 
text itself is neutralized by the fact that after fulfilling its function, 
the text transforms into autonomous understanding. The project is designed 
to be maximally effective in fulfilling its task while remaining 
a temporary construction.
